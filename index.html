Primary school: YinCheng Primary School

YinCheng Primary School is known for its good teachers, beautiful environment and various activities.

I actually enjoyed a good time there... and the experiences there may probably be the best memories in my life.

I could still remember the days we were having Olympic Mathematics together although this test course is abandoned by NFLS today. Well... it was really a pity for me not to pass that test, but I did have a good time with my classmates.

Middle school: Nanjing Jinling Huiwen School

Jinling Huiwen always ranks the first in Nanjing's public schools, and it has a lot of good facilities for students and teachers.

As I failed to pass the entry test to NFLS, I went to Jinling Huiwen School that is near to my home.

These three years in middle school was not that comfortable as my teachers only urgently wanted us to study... but my grade did improved a lot as my teachers were really responsible for us.

I finally succeeded in going to NFLS and continued my dream.

High School: NFLS BCA

NFLS is famous for everything, and it is usually considered as the best high school in Nanjing. BCA is also been held for a lot of years.

These three years are very complex for me... the events that I have experienced change me a lot...

For the first year, I underwent a chaotic life.

For the second year, I underwent an exotic life.

For the third year, I underwent a normal life.

Nothing could conclude the three years... but I do want to thank BCA for letting me grow a lot. I will talk about that more in the school life part.
