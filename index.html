Primary school: YinCheng Primary School

YinCheng Primary School is known for its good teachers, beautiful environment and various activities.

I actually enjoyed a good time there... and the experiences there may probably be the best memories in my life.

I can still remember the days we were having Olympic Mathematics together although this test course is abandoned by NFLS today. Well... it was really a pity for me not to pass that test, but I did have a good time with my classmates.

Middle school: Nanjing Jinling Huiwen School

Jinling Huiwen always ranks first in Nanjing's public schools, and it has a lot of good facilities for students and teachers.

As I failed to pass the entry test to NFLS, I went to Jinling Huiwen School which is near to my home.

These three years in middle school were not that comfortable as my teachers only urgently wanted us to study... but my grade did improve a lot as my teachers were really responsible for us.

I finally succeeded in going to NFLS and continued my dream.

High School: NFLS BCA

NFLS is famous for everything, and it is usually considered the best high school in Nanjing. BCA is also been held for a lot of years.

These three years have been very complex for me... the events that I have experienced changed me a lot...

For the first year, I underwent a chaotic life. As I first touched a brand new mode of learning and living, I felt too nervous about the atmosphere so I found it hard to balance my studying and life... However, luckily, there were people that liked me and treated me well and I felt warm about that.

For the second year, I underwent an exotic life. As I would like to try new things that I have never touched before, I have done a lot of special things like running for the Students' Council or being the vice leader of a club. Although this kind of life was new for me, I spent too much time socializing to keep a good grade. However, I stopped this trend and improved my grade a lot after the midterm exam.

For the third year, I underwent a wonderful life. In order to apply for good universities, I tried my best to improve my grades and I finally got a satisfactory result, but this does not mean I only focused on studying. I became the leader of a club and the teacher's assistant in a Science course as well as meeting and making friends with a lot of people. I also got the honour of the BCA citizenship award... It was absolutely the most successful year of the high school.

Nothing could conclude the three years... but I do want to thank BCA for letting me grow a lot.

Thank you, these excellent years!

University: King's College London

King's College London, which is located in the centre of London, has also brought me excellent memories.

King's College London has beautiful campuses, gentle schoolmates and responsible teachers. Other than studying at King's, I also meet many people who are kind and helpful. I enjoy the life here.

It was indeed a challenge for me to study abroad at the beginning, but as time went on, I got used to life in London and have achieved a balance between study and life.

These university experiences may be the real-time that I live and study on my own. Although I felt it hard in the beginning, the way of getting used to life indeed made me grow up.
